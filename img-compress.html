Frequently Asked Questions

Is this image compressor free to use?

Yes, our image compression tool is completely free to use with no hidden costs or watermarks.

Are my images uploaded to your servers?

No, all processing happens directly in your browser. Your images never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy.

What's the maximum image size I can compress?

You can compress images up to 20MB in size. For best results with very large images, consider resizing them first.

Which format should I choose for best compression?

WebP generally offers the best compression with good quality. For maximum compatibility, JPEG is still the most widely supported.
